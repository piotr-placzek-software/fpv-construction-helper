Drone acceleration refers to how quickly a drone can change its speed. It measures how fast the drone can go from a lower speed to a higher speed or vice versa.

This calculation is pretty simple. You must find motor thrust in manufacturer's specification (in grams) and provide RTF weight of your drone (also in grams)

Acceleration = ((Thrust / 1000) * GRAVITY_CONST * Amount Of Motors) / (Weight / 1000)
